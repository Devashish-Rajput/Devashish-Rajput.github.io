Building the Future with SolarArise

When India started on the solar mission, many skeptics called India's 22GW solar-goal an unachievable dream.They believed fossil fuels could never be replaced.

Today, as India's installed solar capacity exceeds 25GW's, the reality has changed. Solar power is now the least expensive. We stand on the brink of eliminating every polluting fossil-fuel plant in India.

At SolarArise, we've been converting abandoned land into sources of cheap, clean solar power. We own all our plants and keep them operating. We're building a future without fossil fuels, a future where everyone can breathe fresh air.
