    Description

    SolarArise develops, owns and operates grid-connected solar projects in India. It was founded by Tanya Singhal, Anil Nayar, and James Abraham in 2014.The three founders previously worked together at SunBorne Energy before founding SolarArise.

    Solar Arise currently owns and operates 158.7 MW of grid-connected solar power projects in India.

    India has a target of developing 22,000 megawatts of solar power plants and an additional 8,000 megawatts is expected in local generation, bringing the total to 30,000 megawatts by 2022, which was later increased to 100,000 megawatts by the Narendra Modi government in the 2015 Union budget of India.
