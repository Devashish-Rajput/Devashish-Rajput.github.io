What we do

At first blush, solar energy is perhaps the most elegant solution to our energy needs. The sun blasts our planet’s surface with more than enough energy to keep us going forever. So we are here to create solutions for utilising this clean energy. We work with everyone involved in growing solar power in India.

Solar power in India

Solar power in India is a fast developing industry. The country's solar installed capacity reached 28.18 GW as of 31 March 2019. India has become globally the lowest cost producer of solar power.We are ready to involve in this sector to meet grwoing energy demands of India.
