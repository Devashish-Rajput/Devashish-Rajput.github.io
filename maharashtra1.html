Project At A Glance

Project Site: Maharashtra, India

Project Details

  • Location Chatgaon Village, Beed District, State of Maharashtra, India
  • Coordinates 18.961683° N, 76.212849° E
  • Capacity 67.2 megawatt (MWDC) photovoltaic power station
  • CO2 Reduction 41,200 Tonnes P.A.
  • Commissioned 10 August 2017

Description

The solar power plant Maharashtra I at Chatgaon Village, Beed District, State of Maharashtra, India is a 67.2 megawatt (MWDC) photovoltaic power station, commissioned in August 2017.

It was constructed using 102,241 solar modules. It covers an area of 306 acres (124 hectares) and supplies about 126,000 people with energy.
